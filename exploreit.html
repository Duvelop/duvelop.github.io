FAQs

What is ExploreIT?

ExploreIT is a four-day long series of four-hour workshops that Hack Nights will be partnering with UC Irvine to offer in Summer 2017 from June 19 - June 22. The workshops will involve both hardware and software programming. Participants with no knowledge of computer science will learn basic concepts and apply them to a hands-on project. Industry mentors will be on hand to give attendees a glimpse into coding in the workplace and offer insight into being a female in a male-dominated industry.

Who can attend?

Girls who are entering middle school or 9th grade in the 2017-2018 school year. Exceptions may be made on a case-by-case basis. The age limit is necessary due to the hardware that will be used in workshops.

How do I register?

Those who are interested can fill out this form. Applications for the program will open up in Mid-May and those who have filled out the form will receive email updates until then. Space is limited to 30 attendees and we will prioritize those who will be able to attend all four days of the program.

What skills will be taught?

Fundamental coding concepts will be covered first to provide a strong foundation to attendees. The workshops will be fast-paced and very hands-on. Throughout the four days, attendees will have the chance to learn how to code in multiple languages and also learn about different programming paradigms.

What will be provided?

T-shirts, lunch, hardware, and other goodies will be provided.

Does this cost money?

Participation is completely free.
